üì¶ Order Questions

To place an order:

  1. Browse products and add items to your cart
  2. Review your cart and click "Proceed to Checkout"
  3. Sign in to your account or check out as a guest
  4. Enter shipping and payment information
  5. Review your order and click "Place Order"

You'll receive an order confirmation email once your order is successfully placed.

You can modify or cancel your order within 30 minutes of placement:

  • Visit "My Orders" in your account
  • Select the order you want to change
  • Click "Request Modification" or "Cancel Order"

Once your order enters processing, modifications may not be possible. Contact customer support for assistance with orders that can't be modified online.

üöö Shipping Questions

To track your order:

  1. Log in to your account and go to "My Orders"
  2. Find the order you want to track
  3. Click "Track Package"

You can also track without an account using the tracking number sent to your email. Visit our tracking page and enter your tracking number.
